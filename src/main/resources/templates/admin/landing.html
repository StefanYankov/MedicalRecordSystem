Administrator Homepage

Welcome, Admin!

From this central hub, you can manage all aspects of the Medical Record System.

Use the sidebar on the left to navigate to the different management sections:

  • Dashboard: View key metrics and recent activity.
  • Doctors: Manage doctor profiles and specialties.
  • Patients: Manage patient records and insurance status.
  • Visits: View, edit, and delete visit records.
  • Reports: Generate and view system-wide reports.
You have full access to view and edit all information in the system.
